Welcome to Rebirth Our World!!

We are a community service act dedicated to promote ideas of awareness of global issues. Whether it is enviromentally, socially or mentally, we all have the power to create impact! We believe in reducing the stigma around social issues, being more conscious about the way we treat our environment, and overall just spreading good change for our world!!

About us

We are a community service act based in the Bay Area, in Fremont California. Our primarily goal is to spread awareness of social issues that are stigmatized in our society such as self care, mental health awareness, sustainability, and overall promote global issues! Through the impact of social media and hosted events by our organizers, we hope to spread the message of rebirthing our world!

How to Join Our Team

To be a part of our community, make sure to follow us on Instagram @rebirth_our_world, and dm us to be featured in a post! To be an event organizer, feel free to contact us! As a community service act, this organization is for everyone! Regardless of age or gender, we provide a judgment-free community for all! Anyone is eligible to join our team and help spark a movement!
